FAQs and Tutorials

What is Digital Signage?

Digital signage uses TV screens to display content such as images, video, streaming media, and information.

What are the benefits of using digital signage?

Digital Signage allows you to change the content on the screen anytime. Since it is run digitally, no extra costs on paper or plastic based posters and standees.

Who can use BlynQ Digital Signage?

What is a player?

BlynQ-player is an app which plays the scheduled content at your TV screen. It talks with the cloud servers to get the updated schedules and plays it on TV screen.

How to set up a player?

Get an android TV Box. Connect the Box to the TV screen with the HDMI cable. Power-on and connect the device to internet with WIfi Go to Playstore and download the BlynQ-player app. Once installed, open the app and a “screen-key” is displayed on the screen.

How to install updates for player?

You needn’t do anything for updates. BlynQ automatically install the updates whenever a update is available.

Do I need continuous internet connection to use BlynQ?

BlynQ doesn’t need continuous internet connection. It has a smart downloader which downloads and maintains the content at the player side. It clears off the stale content when required.

What are playlists and schedules?

Playlist is a logical group of content items. Schedule is your digital advertisement which you want to publish on the screens. It comprises of the layout, content to be played in each partition, and timeline for selected screens.

What are widgets?

Widgets are apps that simplify the process of displaying RSS tickers, social media content, web urls on TV screen.

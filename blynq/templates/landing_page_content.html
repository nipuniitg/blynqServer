About Us

BlynQ Technologies is breaking the cycle of traditional content display solutions with a robust, scalable, powerful and cost effective technology solution. Incubated with IIT Guwahati Technology Incubation Center, company is started by IIT Guwahati alumni. We aspire to be unique and best providers of digitised content display solutions - conceptually interesting, visually elegant and easy-to-use. We have replaced 100s of physical boards and pen-drive swamped offline content displays with intelligent digitised, internet-enabled and cloud featured solution.

Our mission at BlynQ is to use technology to enable displays with engaging and targeted content. Needless to mention, we are super easy to work with, available 24*7 for all customer queries.

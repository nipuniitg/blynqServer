A powerful display management platform to keep audience informed using digital signage that can enhance experience and create a positive impact.

Product

Create your own channel on your TV screens to communicate better with your audience. We provide a simple and easy to set up cloud based platform with clean and intuitive web interface for creating campaigns to run. This is a good fit for Business Owners looking for easy-to-use and economical solution.
All you need is a digital screen(TV) and a working internet connection. Contact us and get started.

Software components

BlynQ Web-portal - simple and easy-to-use interface to smartly configure and control any number of screens from single location.

Hardware

A Media Player to instantly update and play your campaigns.

Media Player is a small device that plug in to any TVs HDMI port, without any mount or tools. It can be connected to a wireless internet connection or to a LAN (using USB to LAN adapter), for updating your campaigns periodically.

More than Digital Signage

At BlynQ we pursue for efficiency and effectiveness. Our portal provides a clean and intuitive interface that lets any general internet user to easily operate and manage screens effectively. Our media player is optimised for nominal internet usage. Changes done to any screens and content will be reflected immediately within seconds precision. Content is stored locally to use minimal internet data.
Looking for additional services on top of digital signage software licenses? BlynQ can help out with CONTENT and CUSTOMIZATION also. Contact us for more details.

Features

Blynq makes it easier to the user by providing various features to effectively manage their screens.

Cloud Storage

Remote Management of your screens from anywhere and anytime. Access and control all your screens right from the browser.

Dynamic Playlists

Easily create playlists, set order and duration for each media with simple drag-and-drop interface.

Layouts

Select from existing variety of layouts or customise your own layout to run multiple campaigns together on your screen.

Instant Update

Real-time control of TV Screens. Updates to screens content will be pushed within a blink.

Content Library

Upload your content into our cloud and access it from anywhere. Images, video, web links, PDFs and Text scroll are supported.

Schedule

Simple timeline and recurrence features to periodically repeat your campaings.

Preview

Gives a clear picture of how your campaign would look like on your screen to review and take action.

Reports and Analytics

Get a detailed real-time report for tracking active time of screens, which playlists and content are active in any given timeline.

Real Time monitor

Get live status of screens activity at any moment.
